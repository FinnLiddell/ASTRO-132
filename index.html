Class Overview

Embark on a cosmic journey with ASTRO 132! This course delves into the mysteries of the universe, from the explosive births of stars to the enigmatic pull of black holes.

ASTRO 132 is designed for students of all backgrounds who are fascinated by the wonders of the night sky. Through a dynamic combination of lectures, planetarium experiences, and real-world observations, you'll learn the fundamental principles of astrophysics and cosmology. Discover how stars evolve, how galaxies collide, and how the universe itself began and continues to expand.

Professor Mark Reiser brings the cosmos to life with engaging storytelling, vivid demonstrations, and real research opportunities. Students will not only absorb theoretical knowledge but also gain practical experience in astronomical observation techniques and data analysis. Whether you dream of becoming an astrophysicist or simply want to appreciate the universe on a deeper level, ASTRO 132 offers an unforgettable academic adventure.

What you'll experience:

  • Captivating lectures by Professor Mark Reiser
  • Immersive planetarium sessions
  • Hands-on telescope observations

Join ASTRO 132 and let your curiosity guide you across the stars. Your voyage into the unknown starts here.
